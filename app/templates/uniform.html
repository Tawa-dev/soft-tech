Corporate Uniform Manual

The purpose of a uniform is to create a standard look. It is your personal responsibility to appear immaculate while wearing the company uniform.

Days for Wearing Uniform

Monday to Friday.

Saturday – Casual attire.

  • Ladies – No leggings, no miniskirts, no spaghetti tops, no lace and transparent tops.
  • Gents – No rafters, no shorts.

Name badges are compulsory and to be worn from Monday to Friday. Kindly wear them on the left side, above your shirt pocket when not wearing a jacket or on your lapel if wearing a jacket.

Important Notice

  • Staff shall wear uniforms properly, presenting a professional image. Uniforms should be clean and ironed/steamed at all times (please do not wear wrinkled clothes to work!).
  • All alterations to uniforms must be performed by the uniform supplier, 4 MAY.
  • Uniform should be worn with appropriate shoes.
  • The uniform should fit comfortably and not be too tight or too loose.
  • Staff is liable for issued uniform items that have been lost, stolen or damaged.
  • When ironing, place a damp cloth, do not iron direct on fabric to avoid making the fabric shine.
  • Staff must not wear uniforms whilst on leave or weekends.
  • Please do not chew bubble gum whilst wearing uniform, its inappropriate.
  • Staff shall not enter public entertainment areas whilst wearing FMC uniform.
  • Please do not get photographed in FMC uniform for social media use.

Ladies

  • You are not allowed to cut the length of either the dress or skirt (dress/skirt length should always be below the knees.
  • Uniforms should be worn with Flat or Court shoes (moderate heels).
  • Should you decide to wear Panty hose stockings, chocolate and charcoal shades are acceptable. Please do not wear black.
  • Hair must be clean, conservative (please be mindful of the colour of weave and extension that you use) well groomed and styled in a professional way.
  • Earrings - Pair of matching gold or silver tone earrings are acceptable. Dangle and drop style earrings may not exceed 2.5 cm.
  • Make up may be worn and should be compatible with skin colour and appropriate for day time business wear.
  • Nail colour – Dayglo (i.e. bright/neon pink, yellow and orange) are not appropriate colours for the office. If wearing a peep toe shoe, please ensure that your toe nails are presentable and if there is nail colour it should be of the appropriate colour.

Jewellery

  • Necklaces should be worn under blouses.
  • Bangles or bracelets must be slim and discreet – gold/silver.
  • Watches must be discreet and professional.

Shoes

  • Gents: Clean and polished black formal shoes only. No gloss shoes please.
  • Ladies: Black or Navy shoes only. No hooker heels please.

Gents

  • Suits are dry cleanable only. Hand washing is unacceptable.
  • Wear plain navy blue or black socks only.
  • Black formal belts. No fancy buckles.
  • If you cannot keep your hair short, keep it shaved.
  • No jewellery on men except for watches.

Students on Attachments

FMC will not provide you with a uniform but please note that you will are required to wear black bottoms and white tops ONLY i.e.

  • Ladies – black knee length skirt/pants and a white shirt/blouse (no lace / transparent fabric).
  • Gents – Black trousers and white shirt (no necktie).
  • Black shoes only for both.
